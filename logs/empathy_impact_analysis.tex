\subsection{Key Findings}

\begin{itemize}
\item Selfish agents ($\alpha=1.0$) converge near Nash equilibrium welfare
\item Empathic agents ($\alpha<1.0$) achieve significantly higher social welfare
\item Maximum efficiency gain: 98.7\% of optimal
\item Empathy mechanism successfully overcomes the social dilemma
\end{itemize}
